Introduction: The curriculum design concept of this department is to cultivate information engineering talents with professional and problem-solving abilities. In addition to the common courses of the school (foreign language and general studies), the curriculum planning is divided into three parts: professional compulsory courses, professional electives and free electives. The main purpose of the professional required courses is to lay the academic foundation for students to have information engineering; the professional elective courses are to increase the depth and breadth of students in the information field; the free elective courses are reserved for students to be flexible, so that students can freely study this subject according to their own interests.

My Experience: NYCU CS provides extraordinary education in the computer science field, which is the main reason I chose to study here. I am currently in my junior year at NYCU CS, and I have learned a lot of programming skills. I have also improved my presentation and social abilities. I believe NYCU CS offers an ideal environment for students interested in computers.
