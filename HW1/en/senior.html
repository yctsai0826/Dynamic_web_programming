National Hsinchu Girls' Senior High School. Based on the long-standing good tradition of Bamboo Girl and the solid foundation laid by the careful planning of successive directors, although the current student affairs work is complicated, it is more diverse, lively, creative and ingenious. Everything related to students including food, clothing, housing, transportation, education, cleaning and coping, physical and mental exercises to strengthen the body, development and management of student clubs, planning and design of various lectures and extracurricular activities, international education travel, participation in service learning and other activities , we all actively integrate human rights and rule of law education, gender equality education, environmental protection education, and life education. We hope that students will have a modern sense of citizenship and pay attention to social issues; we encourage students to explore their interests, understand themselves, develop diverse perspectives, and cultivate time management through participation. abilities, problem-solving skills, leadership skills, and interpersonal skills to make the best adaptive choice for the future; we also hope that smart and outstanding bamboo women will have the characteristics of "diligence, down-to-earth, positivity, and enthusiasm" to give full play to their strengths, Benefit others and shine at all levels of society.
